ABOUT ME

I am a passionate third-year Computer Science student with a strong focus on web development and UI/UX design. As a full-stack developer, I love bringing ideas to life through intuitive and visually appealing interfaces. My goal is to create seamless digital experiences that not only look great but are also highly functional. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to continuous learning, I'm excited to contribute to projects that make a real impact. I envision my future in a dynamic role where I can combine my technical skills and creativity to drive innovation in the tech industry.
